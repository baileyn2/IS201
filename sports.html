Playoff Predictions

East Champion

Boston Celtics

With Jayson Tatum, returning from injury, the Boston Celtics are back to full health. This team won the championship two years ago, and it wouldn't be surprising to see them go all the way again.

East Champion Sleeper Pick

Cleveland Cavaliers

Not many people are talking about this Cavs team, that just traded for James Harden not too long ago. Adding that playoff experience to a roster full of talent and shooting can be dangerous if teams do not prepare sufficiently.

West Champion

Oklahoma City Thunder

Champions from a year ago, this Thunder team has continued to beat down teams week after week. Even with their in-season struggles against the San Antonio Spurs, it will be hard to take down this time in 7 games, especially since they will hold home-court advantage the entire playoffs

West Champion Sleeper Pick

Minnesota Timberwolves

It's tough to lose when you have the most confident player in the NBA on your team. Though struggling with injury, Anthony Edwards has the intangibles and clutch gene to beat any team in the playoffs. The first round matchup against the Nuggets is a tough one, but if they can get past Jokic, the rest of the league should worry.
